# Product Context: Storyverse

## What is Storyverse?
Storyverse is an AI-powered creative engine that allows users to forge epic stories, complete with generated comic panels and memes. It bridges the gap between text prompts and rich, multimodal storytelling.

## Target Audience
- **Creators & Writers**: Looking for visual inspiration for their stories.
- **Meme Enthusiasts**: Users looking to quickly generate highly shareable, humorous content.
- **RPG Players/DMs**: Gamers who want to visualize their campaigns and characters.

## Key Jobs to be Done
- Effortlessly generate a story from a simple prompt.
- View and read a feed of previously generated stories.
- Consume visually striking comic panels alongside the text.
- Export or view memes generated from the story's context.

## Brand Voice
- **Mystical yet Modern**: We use terms like "Forge", "Citadel", and "Grimoire", but presented through a sleek, hyper-modern, and frictionless interface.
- **Playful**: Embracing the meme-generation aspect with a wink and a nod.
- **Empowering**: Making the user feel like a master creator (a "Paladin" of stories).
